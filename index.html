Some poetry..

Oh freddled gruntbuggly computer coding mess,
Thy micturations are to me,(with big yawning)
As plurdled gabbleblotchits,
On a lurgid server bee,
That mordiously hath blurted out ARRRRRG,
Its earted jurtles, grumbling
Into a rancid festering confectious organ squealer.
[drowned out by the moaning and screaming of bootcampers]
Now the jurpling slayjid agrocrustles,
Are slurping tiptaptaptap hagrilly up the axlegrurts,
And living glupules frart and stipulate,
Like jowling meated liverslime,
Groop, I implore thee, my foonting turlingdromes,
And hooptiously drangle me,
With crinkly bindlewurdles,mashurbitries.
Or else I shall rend thee in the cnm gobberwarts with my blurglecruncheon laptop,
See if I don't!
(I probably won't!)
